What is UNC student tournaments?

UNC student tournaments is a student run organization that hosts a wide variety of sports tournaments throughout the school year. Four students formed the organization because of how disorganized UNC intermurals can be. We hope that Student Tournaments can be a good substitute to those looking for an easy and simple way of playing some good competition. We are community driven and are open to any UNC student as long as they are respectful of others and can play fair. We are all adults now, so live out your March Madness dreams and sign up for a tournament of your liking!

Who can participate?

Any UNC student can participate as long as they have a PID and a UNC email address. Email confirmation will be necessary so that we do not have rogue sign ups. On the sign up page their are a couple extra questions that are used to try and form more competitive teams. Be honest! Don't pull a Kevin Durant!

When?

All gametimes will be listed under the schedule tab and updated accordingly. We work on the clock to make sure that there are never any scheduling problems and if enough participants ask, games can and will be moved, sometimes. Were not perfect, but we like complete tournaments!
